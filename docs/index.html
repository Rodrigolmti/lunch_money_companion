About Lunch Money Companion

Lunch Money Companion is an Android mobile application developed independently by Rodrigo Lopes Martins. This application is designed to enhance your financial management experience by providing convenient access to your Lunch Money financial data.

Key Features:

  • Data Accessibility: Easily access and view your financial data from Lunch Money via the mobile app.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Enjoy a simple and intuitive interface for effortless navigation and data retrieval.
  • Data Privacy: Your privacy is our top priority. The app does not store, collect, or share your personal information.
  • Real-Time Updates: Retrieve real-time financial information directly from Lunch Money's API.
  • Security: Your Lunch Money account credentials are used solely for API access and are not stored by the app.

How to Use Lunch Money Companion:

  1. Download and install the Lunch Money Companion app from the Google Play Store.
  2. Launch the app and log in using your Lunch Money API token.
  3. Explore your financial data, including expenses, income, and budgets, with ease.
  4. Enjoy the convenience of real-time updates and secure access to your financial information.
  5. Rest assured that your personal data is not stored or shared by the application.

Lunch Money Companion simplifies the way you interact with your Lunch Money account, making it easier than ever to manage your finances on the go. Experience the power of seamless access to your financial data while maintaining the highest level of data privacy and security.
